Path to healing our planet

The United Nations has, as part of it's 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development come up with 17 goals. These goals are all vital to stopping climate change, world hunger, inequality and to preserve our planet's ecosystems. The goal that we have chosen to focus on is number 13, which says Take urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts. Our climate change quiz is a way to get children to contribute to this.

Our three goals

  • Inform children about the causes of climate change, biggest pollution sources and what can be done to heal our planet.
  • Inspire children to make positive changes in their personal lives and want to change society for the better.
  • Convince as many parents and teachers as possible to get kids to participate in the #ClimateQuiz
