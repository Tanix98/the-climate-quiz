The Climate Quiz

Have kids go together in groups, and the groups will then win points based on how many correct answers they have. An adult should be around to help each team when needed, but kids should be encouraged to do their best to figure it out for themselves. Each correct answer will give the group 10 points. For each 10 points, the group gets a star sticker on the leaderboard.

Use the #ClimateQuiz hashtag on social media to share your favorite moments with everyone else, so that even more people can get inspired to do this quiz!

Quiz questions and answers

Question

Where do used plastic bottles and metal cans go?

See answer

Answer

They are inserted into a pawn machine so they can be reused later.

Question

Which foods are worst for the environment?

See answer

Answer

Meat (cow and sheep meat are worst), dairy, chocolate (unsustainable types), coffee (unsustainable types) and shrimp (farmed).

Question

What are the biggest clean sources of energy?

See answer

Answer

Solar, wind, geothermal, hydropower, ocean and bio-energy.

Question

What are the biggest polluting sources of energy?

See answer

Answer

Coal, petroleum, natural gas and nuclear.
